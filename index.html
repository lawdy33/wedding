Location

Redondo Beach Historic Library

The Redondo Beach Historic Library is a beautiful beach front wedding venue located in Veteran's Park Redondo Beach. Built in the 1930s this building is a unique combination of Spanish and Dutch architecture. Expansive windows on all four sides of the building allow for breathtaking views of the ocean, a giant Moreton Bay Fig (also a landmark) and the sprawling lawn of Veteran’s Park. The Library retains many of its original amenities such as vintage bookshelves and hanging brass light fixtures creating a unique and enchanting place.

***Directions to the library's address on Google Maps are inaccurate. Follow directions to the venue at this point here or pictured above. The library is adjacent to the Redondo Beach Pier.
