Location

Redondo Beach Historic Library

The Redondo Beach Historic Library is a beautiful beach front wedding venue located in Veteran's Park Redondo Beach. Built in the 1930s this building is a unique combination of Spanish and Dutch architecture. Expansive windows on all four sides of the building allow for breathtaking views of the ocean, a giant Moreton Bay Fig (also a landmark) and the sprawling lawn of Veteranâ€™s Park. The Library retains many of its original amenities such as vintage bookshelves and hanging brass light fixtures creating a unique and enchanting place.

***Directions to the library's address on Google Maps are inaccurate. Follow directions to the venue at this point here or pictured above. The library is adjacent to the Redondo Beach Pier.

Hotels

These are some suggestions for places to stay in the South Bay area. Many people are also searching for Airbnb locations in the Redondo Beach area. Note that Airbnb is not allowed in Hermosa Beach. Be sure to book accomodations soon as rooms will fill up in the summer.

Shade Hotel

Redondo Beach

Bride and Groom will be staying here. This is a higher priced hotel but in a great location.

Crowne Plaza Hotel

Redondo Beach

Another great location near the wedding venue and close to the Redondo Beach Pier. After clicking on the link, the only fields to update is the Check-in and Check-out dates and number of Rooms and then click the Book button.

Holiday Inn Express Hermosa Beach

Hermosa Beach

Location is near the wedding venue but a little further from the beach. Close to nightlife on Pacific Coast Highway. Enter 'Lawson Steinbaugh Wedding' as the Group Code when making a reservation.

The Redondo Beach Hotel

Redondo Beach

Another great location near the wedding venue and close to the Redondo Beach Pier.

Sea Sprite Motel

Hermosa Beach

This motel is for you adventurers. The motel itself is not fancy but the location is amazing right on the Hermosa Beach Strand a few feet from the sand. The motel is close to all the action in Hermosa.
